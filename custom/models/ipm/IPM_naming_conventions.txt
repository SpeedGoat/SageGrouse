Integrated Population Model naming conventions

Order of terms
	- Recruitment, as represented by ratio data (R)
	- Young of year survival (jS)
	- Female Survival (fS)
	- Male Survival (mS)
	- Process Error (pe)
		
Use of values
	c signifies that the model used a single term through time
	n means that the values was considered latent and estimated 
		(e.g. female survival) or it was not used in the model 
		(e.g. young of year survival)
	Process error can take values of pe for when the biological process
		is stochastic or npe for deterministic models
